Currently a PhD candidate at **Monash University Malaysia**, Semiconductor Systems & Devices (SSD) Group, under **Ir. Dr. Patrick W.C. Ho** — building a formal framework for **ternary memristive in-memory computing**: treating the intermediate resistance state as an energy-idle condition rather than a third data symbol.

- 🎓 B.Tech, Computer Science & Engineering — VIT Chennai (CGPA 9.01/10)
- 🏆 APJ Abdul Kalam Award · IEEE YESIST12'24 Finalist
- 📄 2× published IEEE Access papers, several manuscripts in review or revision across neuromorphic computing, thermodynamic systems, and computational biology
- 🤝 Open to research collaboration on neuromorphic hardware, memristive computing, and spiking neural systems
